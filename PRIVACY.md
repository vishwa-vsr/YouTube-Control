# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: July 15, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **YouTube Control: Shorts Blocker & Detox** ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience using our extension.

---

### 1. No Data Collection
We do not collect, store, track, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user information. 
* All operations performed by the extension happen entirely on your local computer.
* No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

---

### 2. Browser Permissions Explained
The extension requests only one browser permission to function:
* **`storage`**: Used solely to save your setting preferences (e.g., whether you turned on "Hide Shorts" or "Blur Thumbnails"). This data is saved locally inside your browser's private storage.

The extension does not request access to read your web activity, history, or personal tabs.

---

### 3. Data Security
Because no data is collected or sent over the internet, your information remains fully secure on your own device. The security of your data depends entirely on the security of your web browser and operating system.

---

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be documented by modifying the "Effective Date" at the top of this file and will be included in new updates of the extension.

---

### 5. Contact & Support
If you have any questions, feedback, or concerns about this privacy policy, please feel free to open a support issue on our public GitHub repository page.
